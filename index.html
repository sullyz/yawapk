Description

You and What ARMy? is a cinematic, slapstick, Kung-Fu, beat-em-up inspired by the so-bad-they're-good Kung-Fu flicks of the 1970's.

Playtime: 15 minutes
Rating: Teen... or children with a sophisticated sense of humor.

 History

You and What ARMy? started as a class project at USC, in the world-renowned Interactive Media and Games Division. Inspired by the wacky, over-the-top nature of 1970's Kung-Fu films, Sully Zack (Designer) and Corvyn Kusuma (Engineer), wanted to create a game that made players laugh from start to finish and inspired a sense of wild badassery. 

Over the 3 month development cycle, Sully and Corvyn collaborated with Carlos Eiene and Beth Pang Yap from Berklee College of Music in Boston to help bring the game to life through a funky original score and hilarious sound effects. 

Sully and Corvyn drew reference from films like Sister Street Fighter (1974), and Drunken Master (1978), to capture the "cinematic feel" of Kung-Fu flicks in You and What ARMy?'s cutscenes, specifically with regards to how shots are framed and how characters are lit.
